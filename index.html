Welcome to WPI Suite!

A Platform for Software Engineering Students and Software Engineering Projects

WPI Suite is a platform for hosting tools for software engineering teams as well as a platform that can be used as a basis for software engineering course projects. WPI Suite has evolved from software engineering course projects at Worcester Polytechnic Institute over several years. The projects were assigned in Professor Gary Pollice's software engineering course, CS3733. The current suite is a complete rewrite and redesign that was created as a pair of senior Major Qualifying Projects (MQPs) during the 2012-13 academic year.
